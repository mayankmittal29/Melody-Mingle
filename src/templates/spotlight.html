Amritpal Singh Dhillon (born 10 January 1993),

known professionally as AP Dhillon, is an Indo-Canadian singer,

rapper and record producer associated with Punjabi music.

Five of his singles have peaked on the Official Charts Company

UK Asian and Punjabi charts, while "Majhail" and "Brown Munde" have topped the chart.

Dhillon, alongside his patners Gurinder Gill, Shinda Kahlon

and Gminxr works as trio under their labell 'Run-Up Records'.
